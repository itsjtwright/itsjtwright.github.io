WRONG DESIGN

We are JT Wright & Tristan Deering, a couple of punks interested in computers, plants, music, fashion, design, baking, crypto, photography, and the inner workings of the world. Based in Erie, Colorado, where we constantly make useless things few people will see. We pride ourselves in doing our own thing, and not caring when people think our work is a bit to out there.
